## Repository Guidelines

- Keep discussions focused on scientific research on the science of morphological transformations.
- Keep contributions evidence-based, reproducible, and scientifically rigorous.
- Ethics, politics, ideology, and public policy are outside the scope of this repository.
- Resolve scientific disagreements through evidence, reproducible results, sound reasoning, and relevant literature rather than appeals to authority or personal opinion.
- Critique ideas, not people. Assume good faith and remain professional.

Maintainers may redirect or close discussions that fall outside the project's scope.
